DESCRIPTION

Chana Ghanta is a hearty and flavorful dish made with chickpeas, assorted vegetables, and aromatic spices. It's a wholesome, one-pot meal that showcases the essence of Jharkhandi cuisine, offering a delightful blend of textures and tastes!

INGREDIENTS

Chana Dal (Split Bengal Gram) - 1 cup
Pumpkin (Diced) - 1 cup
Raw Banana (Diced) - 1 cup
Sweet Potato (Diced) - 1 cup
Turmeric Powder - 1/2 teaspoon
Mustard Oil - 2-3 tablespoons
Panch Phoron (Bengali Five Spice Mix) - 1 teaspoon
Dry Red Chilies - 2-3
Bay Leaf - 1
Ginger (Grated) - 1 teaspoon
Asafoetida (Hing) - 1/4 teaspoon
Salt - to taste
Sugar - 1 teaspoon
Fresh Coconut (Grated) - 2 tablespoons (for garnish)
Ghee (Clarified Butter) - 1 teaspoon (for garnish)

DIRECTIONS

1. Wash the Chana Dal (Split Bengal Gram) thoroughly and soak it in water for about 30 minutes.

2. After soaking, drain the water from the Chana Dal.

3. In a pressure cooker, add the soaked and drained Chana Dal, diced Pumpkin, Raw Banana, and Sweet Potato.

4. Add Turmeric Powder and a pinch of Salt to the ingredients in the pressure cooker.

5. Pressure cook these ingredients until you hear 3-4 whistles. Ensure that the Chana Dal and vegetables are cooked well and become soft.

6. In a separate pan, heat Mustard Oil until it starts to smoke. Then, reduce the heat to medium.

7. Add Panch Phoron, Dry Red Chilies, and Bay Leaf to the hot oil. Allow them to splutter and release their aroma.

8. Add Grated Ginger and Asafoetida (Hing) to the pan. Saut√© for a minute until the ginger becomes aromatic.

9. Pour the cooked Chana Dal and vegetable mixture into the pan with the tempering. Stir well to combine.

10. Add Salt and Sugar to taste. Adjust the seasoning as per your preference.

11. Cook the mixture on low heat for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. This allows the flavors to meld together.

12. Once the Chana Ghanta is ready and has a thick consistency, remove it from the heat.

13. Garnish with freshly grated Coconut and a teaspoon of Ghee (Clarified Butter).

14. Serve hot with steamed rice or roti. Enjoy your authentic Jharkhandi Chana Ghanta.
